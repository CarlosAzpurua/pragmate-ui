### Features @bgroup/ui version 0.0.23

- BeyondIcon supports three different sizes through className, `xs` for `15px`, `md` for `25px` and `lg` for `35px` default is `20px`
- BeyondSppiner supports through parameters the `primary` and `secondary` colors of the project by passing `primary` and `secondary` respectively and you can also pass a custom color through `color`.
- BeyondSppiner supports setting the spinner `size` through a `size` parameter.
- BeyondButton supports through className the `secondary`, `cancel` and `border` buttons.

**For more information visit the documentation at  [beyond-ui.netlify.app](https://beyond-ui.netlify.app) **
